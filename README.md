## Bahay Bites

BahayBites is my own at-home Filipino bakery. I am a student at UNC-Chapel Hill, and food is my passion, and so is coding! 

A little breakdown of what the name means. Bahay means "home" in Tagalog. So its full English name is Home Bites!

This app is the tool I built to help me run it. I am a pre-order only business, so I know exactly how many of each baked good I need for the week. I built this to scale my recipes and figure out exactly what I need to buy and see how much profit I made for the week.

## What It Does This App Do?

- Plug in your weekly preorders and get fully scaled recipes instantly
- Estimates ingredient costs
- Generates a recipe card unique for each item so I know exactly what to make
