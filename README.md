Bahay means "home" in Tagalog — and that's exactly what this is.

BahayBites is my at-home Filipino bakery. I'm Jules, a student at UNC Chapel Hill, 
and I started this because food has always been a huge part of my life. There's 
nothing better than watching someone try Filipino food for the first time and seeing 
their eyes light up. That feeling is everything.

This app is the tool I built to help me run it. Every week I get preorders for 
pandesal, ensaymada, and whatever the weekly special is — and instead of doing the 
math by hand, I built this to scale my recipes and figure out exactly what I need 
to buy. It's my bakery's behind the scenes.

## What It Does

- Plug in your weekly preorders and get fully scaled recipes instantly
- Estimates ingredient costs based on Walmart prices
- Generates a recipe card for each item so I know exactly what to make
